# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making Stratum-ICS a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, discipline, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept and give feedback graciously
- Prioritize the project's goals over personal preferences
- Acknowledge that disagreement is healthy; hostility is not

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith participation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional or academic setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Stratum-ICS spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **conduct@stratum-ics.org** (or via a private GitHub message to a maintainer).

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Violations may result in a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban depending on severity.
